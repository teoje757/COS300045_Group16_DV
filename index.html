Mobile Phone Use While Driving in Australia

Mobile phone fines fell 8.7% (2022–2024) to 330,926, with camera detection (87.6% fines) showing initial spikes in new areas like ACT (700 per 10,000 holders) but major long-term drops in established regions — NSW down 25%, QLD down 41% — proving cameras change driver behavior.
