Key Findings

Camera-based enforcement has proven highly effective in changing driver behavior across Australia. While new enforcement areas initially show high violation rates (ACT: 700 per 10,000 license holders), established programs demonstrate substantial long-term reductions. New South Wales achieved a 25% decrease and Queensland saw a 41% drop in violations, indicating that consistent camera enforcement successfully modifies driver habits over time.
